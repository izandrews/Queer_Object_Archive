Mars (Shoulder Brace):

Iz came to my house to photograph my housemate Ivy. I asked what the project was about. Iz said 'what object would you want someone from the future to find buried with you?' - I chose my shoulder brace, because as someone with a disability it represents my independence and autonomy. It allows me to live when times are hard. It empowers me.
